Tianna Arata

On Tuesday, July 21, Tianna Arata was arrested by SLO PD at about 8 p.m. near Mitchell Park as she and other demonstrators were loading up a car after the protest — which at one point stopped traffic on Highway 101 — and had already dispersed. Tianna’s arrest and charges were unjust. Our community should come together to support her and her family. Protect our young black lives movement leaders right to protest and demand that the district attorney not pursue any charges against Tianna Arata. Our young black leaders deserve to be heard, seen, and supported.

Script: “Hello Representative Carbajal. [State affiliation: I am a resident of San Luis Obispo County, Cal Poly Alumni etc]. I’m calling to ask you to look into the unjust charges filed against 20-year-old Black protest leader Tianna Arata. The police are targeting her in order to stop the local Black Lives Matter Movement. Tianna is facing 8 total charges, including 5 felonies. I know you recently co-sponsored the Justice in Policing Act and I feel this falls under your priorities as our Representative. We need someone to step in and ensure Tianna is not used as a political scapegoat. The local officials are very conservative and will not hesitate to prosecute her to the fullest extent. Please act now.”
