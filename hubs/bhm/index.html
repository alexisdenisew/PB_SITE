Black History Month

Home Take Action Figures, Stories & Orgs Directory

In celebration of Black History Month at PBR, we are shining a spotlight on lesser-known and under-celebrated civil rights leaders and pivotal figures in black history, all while acknowledging the current figures who are actively shaping black history today. Discover fresh graphics featuring these remarkable individuals and other historical stories every week. A new organization making significant contributions in this arena will also be featured here, weekly.

To further honor the legacy of black history, you can utilize our "Take Action" tool, which allows you to advocate for comprehensive black history curriculum in your state by sending automated emails to your local state department of education. Happy Black History Month, let's continue to educate and uplift together!
