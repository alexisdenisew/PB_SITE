Voting Rights

"Around the country we've seen innumerable attacks on the right to vote," said Ronnie Newman, National Political Director at the ACLU. "This year in statehouses across the country, we have witnessed overt efforts to disenfranchise Black, Latinx, and Indigenous voters through attacks on vote by mail, cuts to early voting periods, and unnecessary and burdensome identification requirements. There is a real and present threat to our democracy. Congress and President Biden must pass federal voting rights legislation. There is no time for delay."
